## Overview
This project involved dialect fieldwork across five regions in Shandong Province, including Jinan (*where I did my undergraduate*), Jining, Yantai, Heze, and Tai’an. Through structured interviews and wordlist elicitation, I collected spoken data from different local communities. I then transcribed and organised the data using the [International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Phonetic_Alphabet), focusing on phonetic and lexical variation across regional dialects.
